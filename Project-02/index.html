How to shoot a bow

Materials needed

  • Compound Bow
  • Some arrows
  • A well calibrated pin sight
  • Target
  • Thumb release

Shooting the Bow

  1. Stand with feet shoulder-width apart at a right angle to target.
  2. Grip bow with a calm and loose hand, do not death grip it.
  3. Notch arrow into the rest, and snap it into the notch.
  4. Attach thumb release to the D-loop. DO NOT RELEASE UNTIL YOU ARE READY TO SHOOT.
  5. Pull bow back and plant your hand at an anchor point on your face, generally the corner of your mouth.
  6. Aim the correct pin to your target based on how far you're standing from it.
  7. Slowly lay your finger on the release to shoot, never pull it hard as it will skew your aim.

  8. Check to see where your shot landed! Rinse and repeat!
