Chocolate Pound Cake

Ingredients

  • Nonstick cooking spray
  • 1 tsp instant espresso powder
  • 1 1/2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 1/2 cup boiling water
  • 1 cup dark brown sugar
  • 2 sticks unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flower
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened natural cocoa powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F and spray loaf pan with nonstick cooking spray
  2. Place espresso powder and 3/4 cup chocolate chips in a large bowl. Pour boiling water and let sit for 5 minutes. Whisk until smooth.
  3. Whisk in the brown sugar, melted butter, vegetable oil, and vanilla until smooth. Add eggs and whisk until fully combined.
  4. Add flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt, and sift. Whisk to combine.
  5. Combine the wet and dry ingredients. Use a rubber spatula to fold the flour mixture into the chocolate mixture until just combined.
  6. Add chocolate chips
  7. Scrape batter into loaf pan. Bake until the loaf has risen and cracked a bit on top, 65 to 75 minutes.
  8. Cool and serve the pound cake.
