Why choose compound bows?

I chose to do my project on how to shoot a compound bow because they have been a huge part of my life. Ever since I was young, I would read comics about Green Arrow and would be amazed at how fun it would be to shoot one for fun. Fast forward in time and I started to hunt with a compound bow. Ever since then, I have been in love with bows and spend tons of time out practicing or hunting with them. TV shows like Arrow and Hawkeye have further increased my love for them!
